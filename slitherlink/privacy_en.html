Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to users of the mobile application "Slitherlink" (hereinafter referred to as "this App") provided by overpatch (hereinafter referred to as "the Organization"). It explains how we handle information and data collected through this App and our policy regarding privacy protection.

The Organization respects the privacy of users to the fullest extent and strives to protect user data, understanding the importance of personal information protection.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Collection of Personal Information

This App does not collect direct personal information (name, email address, address, etc.) from users.

1.2 Collection of Usage Information

The Organization may collect data regarding the usage status of this App (hereinafter referred to as "Usage Information") in an anonymized and statistical form. This data is used for the purpose of improving App functions, enhancing user experience, and resolving technical issues. Usage Information may include the frequency of use of App functions, error occurrence status, tendencies in screen transitions, etc., but does not include information that identifies individuals.

1.3 Device Information

If necessary, we may acquire information about the device on which this App is installed (model name, OS version, etc.), but this is also collected in an anonymized form and does not fall under information that identifies individuals.

2. Purpose of Use of Information

The information collected by the Organization is used for the following purposes:

  • Improvement of App functions and enhancement of user experience
  • Resolution of technical issues and provision of support
  • Strengthening of security measures and prevention of unauthorized use

The Organization will not use the information for purposes other than those listed above. Also, we will not use the information for new purposes without the user's consent.

3. Sharing of Information

The Organization will not sell, share, or provide user information to third parties. However, we may exceptionally share information in the following situations:

  • When there is a request for disclosure based on laws and regulations
  • When it is judged necessary for the protection of the user's life, body, or property
  • When it is judged necessary to protect the Organization's rights, property, or services

4. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Currently, this App does not use cookies or other tracking technologies. If we use these technologies in the future, we will update this policy and notify users to that effect.

5. Data Protection

The Organization takes industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or alteration of collected information. However, we do not guarantee complete safety in data communication over the Internet.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

The Organization may update the contents of this policy as necessary. If there are changes, we will notify users by notifying them within the App or posting the latest policy on the Organization's official website. By using this App after the changes, users are deemed to have agreed to the revised Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Information

For questions regarding this policy or consultations regarding the handling of personal information, please contact us at the email address below.

Contact: overpatch.ch@gmail.com
